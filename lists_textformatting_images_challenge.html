Recipe for two minute noodles

Directions

First unpack the noodles from the wrapping. Proceed to add the noodles to pre boiled water. Mix salts and spices. Add to microwave > Wait for two minutes then strain the noodles. Serve in bowl.

Ingredients

  1. Two minute noodles
  2. Boiling water
  3. Spice
